CollegePlus

We embrace new ideas and methods to create new opportunities for clients need to experience the latest technology for professional software products and services. CollegePlus Software Suite is a professional educational software product and solutions application integrated with the management information for a modern educational institution.

  • It contains six software suites Student Management, Human Resource Management, Library Management, Inventory Management, Academic Management and Medical Management.
  • CollegePlus is one-stop solutions for all types of educational institutions like Engineering College and Medical College and Hospital
  • CollegePlus is interoperable, upgradable, and centralized with secured control on the information of the institution
  • CollegePlus also provide accessibility to administrative, operational, academics, knowledge repository and knowledge sharing, it also supports performance monitoring, efficient and quick decision making, co-ordination across various group campus, departments and effective communication with students, staffs, parents and vendors.
