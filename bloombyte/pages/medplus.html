MedPlus

MEDPLUS Software Suite is a professional educational software product and solutions application integrated with the management information for a modern educational institution and hospital requirements. It contains six software suites Medical Management, Student Management, Human Resource Management, Library Management, Inventory Management, Provisional Management.

  • MEDPLUS is one stop solutions for all types of educational institutions like Engineering College and Medical College and Hospital.
  • MEDPLUS has been developed using .NET based technologies for the heterogeneous distributed environment.
  • MEDPLUS is interoperable, upgradable, and centralized with secured control on the information of the institution.
  • MEDPLUS also provide accessibility to administrative, operational, academics, knowledge repository and knowledge sharing, it also supports performance monitoring, efficient and quick decision making, co-ordination across various group campus, departments and effective communication with students, staffs, parents and vendors.
